Computer Literacy Program

In a world of growing technological innovations, children in most developing countries are yet to have access to technical skills. Like most developing countries, Liberia is not an exception. In a research conducted among 280 students in Buchanan, Liberia where we implement most of our ideas, only 25.2% of students can operate a computer while 85% of those respondents have never owned a computer before. We solve this problem by providing students with computer literacy programs during the summer to prepare them for post-high school life.

Interactive STEM quizzing challenges
